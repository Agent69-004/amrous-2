Story

A sanctuary in the Atlas

Nestled at Km 6 Route de Meknès, just outside Azrou, Amrous‑Dar is a retreat where cedar forests whisper through every season. A place of authenticity, warmth, and quiet luxury.

We offer a Moroccan welcome with modern comforts — a seasonal pool, gardens, a restaurant blending local and Italian flavours, and attentive service around the clock.

— Where every stay becomes a story.

Dining

Authentic Moroccan Cuisine

Our on-site restaurant serves traditional Moroccan dishes prepared with fresh, local ingredients. From fragrant tagines to fluffy couscous, every meal is a celebration of flavor.

Start your day with a continental or halal breakfast, and return in the evening for a memorable dinner under the stars on our terrace.

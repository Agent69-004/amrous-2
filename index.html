About Us

Your Home in the Middle Atlas

Nestled along the scenic Route de Meknès, just 6 km from the heart of Azrou, Amrous Dar offers a peaceful escape surrounded by nature. Our 2-star boutique hotel combines traditional Moroccan charm with modern amenities.

Whether you're exploring the cedar forests, visiting nearby Ifrane, or simply unwinding by our seasonal outdoor pool, we ensure every stay is memorable.

  • Seasonal Outdoor Pool
  • Free Private Parking
  • Garden & Terrace
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • 24-Hour Front Desk

Dining

Authentic Moroccan Cuisine

Our on-site restaurant serves traditional Moroccan dishes prepared with fresh, local ingredients. From fragrant tagines to fluffy couscous, every meal is a celebration of flavor.

Start your day with a continental or halal breakfast, and return in the evening for a memorable dinner under the stars on our terrace.

Breakfast: 7:00 – 10:00
Dinner: 19:00 – 22:00
Terrace & Lounge: All day
